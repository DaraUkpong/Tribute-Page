Air Commodore Idongesit Nkanga

A man who contributed heavily to Akwa Ibom's success.


  • 1952 - Born on the 27th of January 1952, at Ikot Nya, Nsit Ibom, AkwaIbom State, Nigeria.

  • 1990 - Sworn-In as Governor of AkwaIbom State on the 5th of September 1990.

  • 1991 - As Wing Commander commissioned the Akwa Ibom State Broadcasting Corporation on the 27th of July 1991.

  • 2001 - Became a board member of the Coooerative Development Bank

  • 2002 - He sought a candidates position in The NDP for the 2003 Governorship elections.

  • 2007 - Appointed Chairman of the Akwa Ibom Airport Implementation Committee. Th Airport later opened on the 26th of November 2009.

  • 2009 - As an elder of the Ibibio People, he was a strong supporter of AkwaIbom state Governor Godswill Akpabio.

  • 2010 - Member of the South-South Elders forum, Discussing the issue of handing over from ailing president Umaru Yar'Adua to Vice President Goodluck Jonathan

  • 2020 - Died due to Covid-19 Complications.

"The late Nkanga was unwaveringly committed and dedicated to the founding ideals and objectives of the group to foster unity, peace and development in the Niger Delta region. He was a selfless, humane, broad-minded, trusted leader, esteemed compatriot and statesman"

------Former Nigerian Vice President, Atiku Abubakar
